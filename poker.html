How to Play:

  • Select cards by clicking on them - selected cards get a yellow border
  • Use Discard to replace selected cards (3 discards per round max)
  • Use Submit Hand when you're happy with your selection
  • If you select specific cards before submitting, only those will be scored
  • If no cards are selected when submitting, your entire hand will be scored
  • Try to beat the target score to advance to the next round!
